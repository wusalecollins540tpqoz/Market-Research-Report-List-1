<p><p>Almond powder is available in two main types: Blanched Almond Powder and Natural Almond Powder. Blanched Almond Powder is made from almonds that have had their skins removed, resulting in a smoother and lighter colored powder. Natural Almond Powder, on the other hand, is made from almonds with their skins intact, providing a more robust flavor and darker color. Both types are commonly used in baking, cooking, and as a nutritional supplement due to their high protein and healthy fat content.</p></p>
