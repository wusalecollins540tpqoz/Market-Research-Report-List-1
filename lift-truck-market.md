<p><p>Class 1 forklift trucks are electric motor rider trucks used indoors and powered by batteries, ideal for warehouses. Class 2 forklift trucks include electric motor narrow aisle trucks, order pickers, and electric motor hand trucks, suitable for narrow warehouse aisles. Class 3 forklift trucks are electric motor hand trucks or hand/rider trucks, often used for pallet jacks. Class 4/5 forklift trucks are internal combustion engine trucks used for heavy-duty outdoor applications, such as loading and unloading shipping containers.</p></p>
