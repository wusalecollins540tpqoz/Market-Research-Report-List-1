<p><p>3D Printing Gases Market comprises three main types: Argon, Nitrogen, and Gas Mixtures. Argon is commonly used in metal 3D printing processes due to its inert properties. Nitrogen is used in selective laser sintering and stereolithography to prevent oxidation during printing. Gas Mixtures, such as argon/helium or nitrogen/hydrogen, are used in specific applications to improve print quality and performance. These gases play a crucial role in ensuring the desired results in various 3D printing processes.</p></p>
