Transmission technology sends a signal from the key fob to the vehicle via radio waves, allowing for remote unlocking and starting. Other types may include systems that use Bluetooth or biometric authentication for access. These innovative technologies enhance convenience, security, and user experience in modern automotive systems.</p></p>
