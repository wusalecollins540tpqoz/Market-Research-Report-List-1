<p><p>Fiberglass sheets are widely used in various industries and can be classified into two main types: flat sheets and corrugated sheets. Flat fiberglass sheets are smooth and have a uniform surface, making them ideal for applications where a smooth finish is needed. On the other hand, corrugated fiberglass sheets have a wavy pattern that provides added strength and rigidity, making them suitable for roofing and siding applications. Both types of fiberglass sheets are popular in the construction, automotive, and aerospace industries.</p></p>
