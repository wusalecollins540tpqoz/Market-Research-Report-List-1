<p><p>Automotive valves market consists of various types such as solenoid valves, EGR valves, brake combination valves, AT control valves, and engine valves. Solenoid valves regulate the flow of fluid or gas in the vehicle. EGR valves control the exhaust gas recirculation process. Brake combination valves ensure proper distribution of brake fluid. AT control valves manage the automatic transmission system. Engine valves help in controlling the flow of air and fuel into the engine cylinders. Each type serves a specific function in the overall operation of the vehicle.</p></p>
