<p><p>The Wireless Handheld Spectrometer market includes various types such as FTIR (Fourier-transform infrared spectroscopy), NIR (near-infrared spectroscopy), Raman spectroscopy, and others. FTIR spectrometers analyze the absorption or emission of infrared light to identify substances. NIR spectrometers use near-infrared light to analyze chemical compositions. Raman spectrometers use Raman scattering to identify materials based on their molecular vibrations. Other types may include UV-Vis (ultraviolet-visible spectroscopy) or fluorescence spectroscopy. Each type offers unique capabilities for different applications in industries such as pharmaceuticals, agriculture, and environmental monitoring.</p></p>
