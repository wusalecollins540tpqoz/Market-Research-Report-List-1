<p><p>The Automotive Oil Pressure Control Valves market includes various types such as Sliding-Stem Globe Type, V-Notch Ball Type, Butterfly Type, and Angle Type. Sliding-Stem Globe Type valves use a linear motion stem for control, V-Notch Ball Type valves have a V-shaped notch on the ball for precise flow control, Butterfly Type valves have a rotating disc for regulating flow, and Angle Type valves have a 90-degree angled body for specific applications. Each type offers unique features and benefits for controlling oil pressure in automotive systems.</p></p>
