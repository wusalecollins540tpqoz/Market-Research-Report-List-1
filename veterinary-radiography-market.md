<p><p>Veterinary Radiography Market Types include Digital X-ray and Analog X-ray. Digital X-ray technology uses digital sensors to capture images, providing higher quality images in less time compared to Analog X-ray. It also allows for easier storage, retrieval, and sharing of images. Analog X-ray, on the other hand, uses traditional film to capture images. While it is less expensive upfront, Analog X-ray requires more time for processing and can result in lower image quality compared to Digital X-ray.</p></p>
