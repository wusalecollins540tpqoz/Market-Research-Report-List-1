<p><strong>The Xenon Cold Light Source Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Long Arc</li><li>Short Arc</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Xenon cold light sources are commonly categorized into two types: Long Arc and Short Arc. Long Arc sources have a longer distance between the cathode and anode, allowing for a wider beam and higher brightness. They are ideal for applications requiring intense light output, such as medical endoscopy. Short Arc sources have a shorter distance between the cathode and anode, producing a more concentrated and focused beam of light. They are commonly used in applications like microscopy and projection systems.</p></p>
