<p><p>Synthetic rubber coated fabrics are categorized into different types based on the type of rubber used in their coating. These types include Neoprene, Silicone Rubber, Butyl Rubber, Nitrile Rubber, EPDM Rubber, and others. Each type offers unique properties such as durability, flexibility, chemical resistance, and weather resistance. Neoprene is known for its resilience and water resistance, Silicone Rubber offers high heat resistance, Butyl Rubber provides excellent air and gas impermeability, Nitrile Rubber is oil-resistant, EPDM Rubber offers excellent weather resistance, and other types cater to specific applications.</p></p>
