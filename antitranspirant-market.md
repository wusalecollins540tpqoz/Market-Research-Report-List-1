<p><ul><li>Film-forming Type</li><li>Metabolic Inhibitors Type</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Antitranspirants are used to reduce the rate of transpiration in plants. The Film-forming type creates a thin film on plant surfaces, reducing water loss. Metabolic Inhibitors type work by interfering with the metabolic processes in plant cells, leading to reduced transpiration. Other types of antitranspirants in the market may include substances that block stomatal openings or alter the structure of plant cuticles to limit water loss. Each type offers a unique approach to managing transpiration and can be chosen based on specific plant needs.</p></p>
