<p><p>Non-pneumatic tires, also known as airless tires, are a type of tire that does not require air pressure to support the vehicle load. Non-pneumatic tires are particularly useful in applications where punctures and leaks are a common issue, such as in military vehicles, construction equipment, and off-road vehicles.</p><p>The Non-Pneumatic Tires Market is experiencing significant growth, with a projected CAGR of 11.3% during the forecast period.
