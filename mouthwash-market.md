<p><ul><li>Therapeutic Mouthwash</li><li>Cosmetic Mouthwash</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Therapeutic mouthwash is designed to help treat oral health conditions such as gingivitis, plaque buildup, and bad breath. It often contains active ingredients like fluoride, antiseptics, and antibacterial agents. On the other hand, cosmetic mouthwash focuses more on freshening breath and providing a pleasant taste, without necessarily treating specific dental issues. It often contains ingredients like essential oils, flavorings, and breath fresheners. Both types of mouthwash play a role in overall oral care, with therapeutic mouthwashes addressing specific problems and cosmetic mouthwashes providing a more enjoyable user experience.</p></p>
