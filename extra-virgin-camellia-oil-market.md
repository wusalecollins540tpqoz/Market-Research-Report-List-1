<p><p>Extra Virgin Camellia Oil is produced through different methods such as expelling and lixiviation process. Expelling involves using mechanical pressing to extract the oil from camellia seeds, resulting in a high quality oil with minimal processing. Lixiviation process, on the other hand, involves using a solvent to extract the oil from the seeds. Both methods have their own advantages in terms of quality and yield, catering to different preferences in the market for extra virgin camellia oil.</p></p>
