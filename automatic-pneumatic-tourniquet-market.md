<p><ul><li>Single Channel</li><li>Dual Channel</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automatic Pneumatic Tourniquet market offers two main types: Single Channel and Dual Channel. Single Channel tourniquets utilize a single pressure channel, providing consistent pressure for medical procedures. On the other hand, Dual Channel tourniquets feature two separate pressure channels, allowing for independent control and monitoring of pressure in different parts of the body simultaneously. Both types cater to the diverse needs of medical professionals, offering precise and efficient pneumatic tourniquet systems for enhancing patient care and surgical outcomes.</p></p>
