Front-end ISP chips focus on image processing, while front-end IPC SoC chips are designed for network cameras. Back-end DVR SoC chips are used in digital video recorders, and back-end NVR SoC chips are utilized in network video recorders. Each type serves a specific function in the video surveillance system, catering to different needs of the market.</p></p>
