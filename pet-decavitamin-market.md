<p><p>The Pet Decavitamin market is divided into two main types based on the type of vitamins they contain: fat-soluble vitamins and water-soluble vitamins. Fat-soluble vitamins include vitamins A, D, E, and K, which are absorbed and stored in fat tissues. They play essential roles in maintaining healthy skin, coat, vision, and bone health. Water-soluble vitamins, such as vitamin C and the B-complex vitamins, are not stored in the body and need to be replenished regularly through diet. They support various bodily functions, including energy production, immunity, and nervous system health.</p></p>
