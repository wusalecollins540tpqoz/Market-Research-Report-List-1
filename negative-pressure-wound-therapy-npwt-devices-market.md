<p><p>Negative Pressure Wound Therapy (NPWT) devices are medical devices used in the treatment of chronic and acute wounds. They apply sub-atmospheric pressure to the wound bed, which helps in promoting healing, reducing edema, and minimizing infection risks. These devices typically consist of a vacuum pump, a foam or gauze dressing, and a collection canister.</p><p>The global NPWT devices market is projected to experience substantial growth during the forecast period. This growth is mainly attributed to the rising prevalence of chronic diseases, such as diabetes and obesity, that increase the incidence of chronic wounds. Additionally, an aging population and the increasing number of surgical procedures further fuel market growth as NPWT devices are widely used in wound management post-surgery.</p><p>The market is also witnessing various technological advancements and product innovations, which contribute to its growth.
