<p><p>Fall arrest safety nets are essential for preventing falls and protecting workers at heights. There are two main types of safety nets available in the market - Knotted Safety Net and Knotless Safety Net. Knotted safety nets are made with intersecting ropes that are tied together, providing a sturdy and durable option for fall protection. On the other hand, knotless safety nets are manufactured with interwoven ropes that create a smoother surface, reducing the risk of snagging and creating a more comfortable working environment. Both types of safety nets are crucial in ensuring workplace safety and compliance with regulations.</p></p>
