<p><p>Tennis Vibration Dampeners come in two main types - the "Button" Dampener and the "Worm" Dampener. The Button Dampener is a small circular accessory that is placed in between the strings of the tennis racket to reduce vibrations upon impact with the ball. On the other hand, the Worm Dampener is a longer, cylindrical-shaped accessory that serves the same purpose of absorbing vibrations during play. Both types of dampeners help minimize discomfort and improve control for tennis players during their games.</p></p>
