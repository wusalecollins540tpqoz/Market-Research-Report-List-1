<p><p>RSU market refers to the market for Roadside Units, which are devices installed along highways to communicate with vehicles and facilitate electronic toll collection (ETC). OBU market refers to the market for On-Board Units, devices installed in vehicles to communicate with RSUs and transmit payment information for toll collection. Both RSUs and OBUs are crucial components of ETC systems, allowing for seamless and efficient toll collection processes. The growth of these markets is driven by the increasing adoption of ETC technology worldwide.</p></p>
