<p><p>Coin cell batteries are categorized into different types based on their chemical composition and intended use. Lithium button cell batteries (CR) are commonly used in small electronic devices due to their high energy density. Silver oxide batteries (SR) are often used in watches and calculators for their long-lasting power. Alkaline button cell batteries (LR) are more cost-effective and commonly used in various household devices. Zinc-air batteries (Zinc-Air) are commonly used in hearing aids. Other types include mercury-free and rechargeable coin cell batteries.</p></p>
