<p><p>Composition resistors are a type of fixed resistor commonly used in electrical circuits. There are three main types of composition resistors on the market: carbon-composition resistors, carbon-film resistors, and metal-film resistors. Carbon-composition resistors are made of a mixture of carbon particles and a binder, offering low cost and high precision. Carbon-film resistors have a carbon film deposited on a ceramic rod, providing stability and accuracy. Metal-film resistors have a thin metal film deposited on a ceramic substrate, offering high precision and stability.</p></p>
