<p><p>Marine antifouling paints market includes various types such as Self-Polishing Copolymer (SPC) Coatings, which release biocides slowly as the paint dissolves; Fouling Release Coatings (FRC), which utilize a slippery surface to prevent marine organisms from attaching; Ablative Type paints, which wear away over time to reveal fresh biocides; and other specialty coatings tailored to specific marine environments. Each type offers unique advantages in preventing the growth of organisms that can decrease ship performance and efficiency.</p></p>
