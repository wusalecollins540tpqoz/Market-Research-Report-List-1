<p><p>Cutting mat market is divided into two main types: Hard-surface (self-sealing) Mats and Self Healing Mats. Hard-surface mats feature a durable surface that does not cut through easily, making them ideal for heavy-duty cutting tasks. On the other hand, self-healing mats are constructed from a unique material that has the ability to seal itself after being cut, prolonging the lifespan of the mat.
