<p><p>Xtrinsic Digital Pressure Sensors come in three main types - Relative, Absolute, and Differential. </p><p>Relative Digital Pressure Sensors measure pressure relative to a reference pressure, making them ideal for applications where baseline pressure is important. </p><p>Absolute Digital Pressure Sensors measure pressure relative to a perfect vacuum, providing accurate readings in environments with changing atmospheric pressures. </p><p>Differential Digital Pressure Sensors measure the difference in pressure between two points, allowing for precise measurements in systems with varying pressures. Each type of sensor has unique applications and advantages in different industries.</p></p>
