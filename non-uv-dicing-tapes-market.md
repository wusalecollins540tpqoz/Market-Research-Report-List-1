<p><p>Non-UV dicing tapes are available in various types such as PVC, PET, PO, and other materials. PVC tapes are known for their high adhesion properties, while PET tapes offer excellent mechanical strength and stability. PO tapes are preferred for their low outgassing properties, making them suitable for cleanroom applications. Other materials used in non-UV dicing tapes include polyimide and polyester, each offering unique features for specific dicing applications. Manufacturers choose the type of tape based on the requirements of the dicing process and the materials being processed.</p></p>
