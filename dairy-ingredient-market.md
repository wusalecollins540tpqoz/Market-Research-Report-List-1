<p><p>The dairy ingredient market can be divided into two main types: milk ingredients and whey ingredients. Milk ingredients include products like skimmed milk powder, whole milk powder, and condensed milk, which are derived from processing milk. Whey ingredients, on the other hand, are derived from whey, a byproduct of cheese production, and include products like whey protein concentrate and whey protein isolate. Both milk and whey ingredients are widely used in the food and beverage industry for their nutritional value and functional properties.</p></p>
