Single-speed transmission is commonly found in simpler EV models, while multi-speed transmission is typically used in high-performance or heavy-duty EVs. Both types cater to different market needs, with single-speed transmission being more cost-effective and easier to operate, while multi-speed transmission offers enhanced driving dynamics.</p></p>
