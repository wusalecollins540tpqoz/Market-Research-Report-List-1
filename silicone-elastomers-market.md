<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Silicone Elastomers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>High Temperature Vulcanised (HTV)</li><li>Room Temperature Vulcanised (RTV)</li><li>Liquid Silicone Rubber (LSR)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Silicone elastomers can be categorized into three main types based on their curing process: High Temperature Vulcanised (HTV), Room Temperature Vulcanised (RTV), and Liquid Silicone Rubber (LSR). HTV elastomers require heat for curing, RTV elastomers cure at room temperature with the help of a curing agent, and LSR elastomers are liquid materials that cure into a solid form when exposed to heat. Each type has specific applications and properties that make them suitable for various industries.</p></p>
