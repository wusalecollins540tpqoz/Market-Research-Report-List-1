<p><ul><li>Vertical Type</li><li>Horizontal Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Rubber injection molding machinery can be categorized into two main types: vertical and horizontal. Vertical machines have a vertical clamping unit and are ideal for precision molding of small parts. Horizontal machines, on the other hand, have a horizontal clamping unit and are better suited for larger parts and high-volume production. Both types of machines offer unique advantages based on the specific requirements of the molding process, making them suitable for a wide range of applications in the rubber industry.</p></p>
