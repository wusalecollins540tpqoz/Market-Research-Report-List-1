<p><p>Captive power plants are facilities that generate electricity primarily for the use of a specific industry or entity. There are four main types of captive power plants: Diesel, Gas, Coal, and Others. Diesel captive power plants utilize diesel generators to produce electricity, Gas plants use natural gas or LPG as fuel, Coal plants use coal as their primary fuel source, and Others consist of alternative sources such as wind, solar, or biomass. Each type of captive power plant has specific characteristics and advantages based on their fuel source.</p></p>
