<p><p>High dielectric plastic films are widely categorized into various types such as PTEF, PET, PP, and others. PTEF films, also known as Teflon, are known for their high chemical resistance and low dielectric loss properties. PET films offer good dielectric strength and moisture resistance. PP films are highly resistant to chemicals and have good mechanical properties. Other types of high dielectric plastic films include PVC, PVDF, and polycarbonate films, each with their unique properties and applications in various industries.</p></p>
