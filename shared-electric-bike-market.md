<p><p>Shared electric bike markets can be categorized into two main types: Point-to-Point and Distributed. In Point-to-Point markets, users can pick up and drop off bikes at designated stations throughout the city, allowing for convenient and organized bike sharing services. On the other hand, Distributed markets do not have fixed stations, allowing users to pick up and drop off bikes at any location within a specified area. This gives users more flexibility in their travels, but may require more effort in locating available bikes.</p></p>
