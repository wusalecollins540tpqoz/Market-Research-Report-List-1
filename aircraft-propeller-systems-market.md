<p><p>Fixed pitch propeller systems have blades that are fixed in one position and cannot be adjusted during flight. These systems are simple in design and are typically used in smaller aircraft. Varying pitch propeller systems have blades that can be adjusted to change the angle of attack and optimize performance at different speeds and altitudes. These systems are more complex and offer greater efficiency, making them suitable for larger aircraft and high-performance applications.</p></p>
