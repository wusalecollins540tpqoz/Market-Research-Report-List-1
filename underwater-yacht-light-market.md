<p><ul><li>Through-hull</li><li>Surface-mount</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Underwater yacht lights come in two main types: through-hull and surface-mount. Through-hull lights are installed by drilling a hole in the hull of the yacht and mounting the light fixture through it, which provides a sleek and seamless look. Surface-mount lights are installed directly on the surface of the yacht's hull, making them easier to install and maintain. Both types of lights enhance the aesthetic appeal of the yacht while also providing functional lighting for nighttime cruising or underwater visibility.</p></p>
