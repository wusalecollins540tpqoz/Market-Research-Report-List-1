<p><ul><li>Adhesive-bonded CLT</li><li>Mechanically Fastened CLT</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Cross Laminated Timber (CLT) is a type of engineered wood product made by bonding layers of solid-sawn lumber together to create strong and stable panels. In the adhesive-bonded CLT market, the layers are bonded together using adhesives, resulting in a seamless and uniform construction. In the mechanically fastened CLT market, the layers are connected using mechanical fasteners such as screws or nails, providing a faster and potentially more cost-effective method of assembly. Both types of CLT offer sustainable and durable construction solutions for various building projects.</p></p>
