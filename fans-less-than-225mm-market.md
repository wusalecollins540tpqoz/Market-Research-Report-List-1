<p><p>Fans less than 225mm in size generally fall into two main market types: axial fans and centrifugal fans. Axial fans are designed to move air parallel to the fan's axis, making them ideal for applications requiring high airflow at low pressures, such as cooling electronic components. Centrifugal fans, on the other hand, move air perpendicular to the fan's axis, making them better suited for applications requiring higher pressures, like HVAC systems. Both types of fans are commonly used in various industrial, commercial, and residential settings for ventilation, cooling, and air circulation purposes.</p></p>
