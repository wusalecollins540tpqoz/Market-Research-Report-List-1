<p><p>Recycled polyester staple fibre is widely used in various markets, including solid fiber and hollow fiber. Solid fiber is densely packed and used in applications like clothing, home textiles, and industrial fabrics. Hollow fiber, on the other hand, has hollow spaces within the fiber, making it lightweight and suited for insulation and padding materials. Both types of recycled polyester staple fiber offer sustainable alternatives to virgin polyester, reducing the environmental impact of textile production.</p></p>
