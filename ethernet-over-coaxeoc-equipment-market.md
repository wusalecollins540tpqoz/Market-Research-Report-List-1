<p><p>Ethernet Over Coax (EoC) equipment market can be categorized into two types: Passive EoC and Active EoC. Passive EoC utilizes existing coaxial cabling infrastructure without the need for additional power sources, making it a cost-effective solution for extending Ethernet network. On the other hand, Active EoC requires powered devices to boost signal strength and extend the reach of Ethernet signals over coaxial cable. Both types offer advantages and serve different purposes based on the specific requirements of the network infrastructure.</p></p>
