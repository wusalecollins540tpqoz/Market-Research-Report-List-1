<p><p>A digital oscilloscope is a type of sampling oscilloscope that converts incoming analog signals into digital data for analysis and display. It offers advanced features like signal storage, automatic measurements, and waveform analysis. On the other hand, an analog oscilloscope uses continuous waveforms to display signals in real-time. While digital oscilloscopes are more commonly used due to their versatility and accuracy, analog oscilloscopes are still preferred by some for their simplicity and ease of use. Both types have their own set of advantages and applications in the market.</p></p>
