<p><ul><li>Casting</li><li>Forging</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Aluminium alloy wheels are mainly categorized into three types within the market: casting, forging, and other. Casting involves pouring molten aluminum into a mold to create the wheel. Forging, on the other hand, involves shaping solid aluminum by applying pressure. The 'other' category may include wheels made through processes like spinning or flow forming. Each type has its own advantages and characteristics, making them suitable for different applications in the automotive industry.</p></p>
