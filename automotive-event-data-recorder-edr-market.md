<p><p>The Automotive Event Data Recorder (EDR) market consists of two main types: Portable Event Data Recorders and Integrated DVD Event Data Recorders. Portable EDRs are standalone devices that can be easily moved from one vehicle to another, providing flexibility and convenience for users. Integrated DVD EDRs are built directly into the vehicle's existing DVD system, offering a more seamless integration and potentially more advanced features. Both types of EDRs are designed to record and store critical data in the event of a crash or other incident for analysis and investigation purposes.</p></p>
