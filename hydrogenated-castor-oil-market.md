<p><p>Hydrogenated Castor Oil market offers three main types: PEG-40, PEG-60, and PEG-80. These types refer to the degree of ethoxylation, which is the process of adding ethylene oxide to the oil. PEG-40 has 40 units of ethylene oxide added, PEG-60 has 60 units, and PEG-80 has 80 units. Each type offers varying solubility and emulsifying properties, making them suitable for different applications in industries such as cosmetics, personal care, pharmaceuticals, and industrial products.</p></p>
