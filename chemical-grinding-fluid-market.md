<p><strong>The Chemical Grinding Fluid Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Mechanical Action Polishing Fluid</li><li>Chemical Action Polishing Fluid</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Chemical grinding fluids are categorized into two main types based on their polishing mechanisms: Mechanical Action Polishing Fluid and Chemical Action Polishing Fluid. Mechanical action polishing fluids contain abrasive particles that physically grind and remove material from the workpiece surface. On the other hand, chemical action polishing fluids rely on chemical reactions to dissolve or breakdown material on the surface. Both types of fluids play a crucial role in enhancing the efficiency and quality of grinding processes in various industries.</p></p>
