<p><p>Edge Emitting Lasers (EEL) are a type of semiconductor laser where the active region is placed at the edge of the laser diode. There are four main types of EELs in the market: Distributed Feedback Laser, Distributed Bragg Reflector Laser, Fabry-Perot Laser, and Broad Area Laser Diodes. Distributed Feedback and Distributed Bragg Reflector lasers offer narrower linewidths and higher coherence, Fabry-Perot lasers provide broader emission spectra, and Broad Area Laser Diodes offer higher output power but with lower beam quality.</p></p>
