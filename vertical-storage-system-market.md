<p><p>Vertical Storage System Market consists of two main types: Vertical Lift Module (VLM) and Vertical Carousel. VLMs are automated storage systems that use trays or carriers to store and retrieve items vertically within a unit. They offer high storage density and improve picking accuracy and efficiency. Vertical Carousels are rotating shelves that bring items to the operator for picking. They offer compact storage solutions for small parts and high-speed retrieval. Both systems optimize space utilization and streamline inventory management processes.</p></p>
