<p><p>Automotive Lamp Systems market can be divided into several types based on their functions:</p><p>1. Forward Illumination: These lamps provide front lighting for safe driving at night or in low visibility conditions.</p><p>2. Signal and Identification Light: These lamps indicate the vehicle's intentions such as turning, braking, or reversing.</p><p>4. Interior and Convenience Light: These lamps provide interior illumination and enhance the driver's comfort.</p><p>5. Others: This category includes specialty lamps for specific purposes such as fog lights or daytime running lights.</p></p>
