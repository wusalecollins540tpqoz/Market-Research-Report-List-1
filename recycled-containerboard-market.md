<p><p>Recycled containerboard refers to paperboard made from recycled fibers and is commonly used in packaging. There are two main types: linerboard and corrugating medium. Linerboard is the flat facing that provides the outer layers of a corrugated box, offering strength and protection. Corrugating medium, on the other hand, is the wavy layer that provides cushioning and rigidity to the box. Both linerboard and corrugating medium play a crucial role in the strength and durability of recycled containerboard packaging.</p></p>
