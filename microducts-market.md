<p><strong>The Microducts Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Direct Install Type</li><li>Direct Burial Type</li><li>Flame Retardant Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Microducts are used for the installation of fiber optic cables and come in different types. The Direct Install Type microducts are designed to be installed inside existing ducts or conduits. Direct Burial Type microducts are meant to be directly buried underground without the need for additional protection. Flame Retardant Type microducts are specially designed to inhibit the spread of fire in case of an emergency. Each type serves a specific purpose and is chosen based on the specific installation requirements of the project.</p></p>
