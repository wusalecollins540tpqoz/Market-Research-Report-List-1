<p><ul><li>Rigid 1-2Sided</li><li>Standard Multilayer</li><li>HDI/Microvia/Build-Up</li><li>IC Substrate</li><li>Flexible Circuits</li><li>Rigid Flex</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Printed circuit boards (PCBs) come in various types to meet different design requirements. Rigid 1-2Sided PCBs are the most common and cost-effective type for simple electronic devices. Standard Multilayer PCBs are used for complex electronic systems with multiple layers. HDI/Microvia/Build-Up PCBs are high-density boards for advanced applications. IC Substrate PCBs are specifically designed for integrated circuits. Flexible Circuits offer flexibility for compact and bendable devices. Rigid Flex PCBs combine the benefits of both rigid and flexible boards. Other PCB types cater to specific niche markets and specialized applications.</p></p>
