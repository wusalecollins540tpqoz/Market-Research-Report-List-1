There are two main types of Alnico magnets on the market: sintered Alnico and cast Alnico. </p><p>Sintered Alnico magnets are made through a powder metallurgy process, resulting in a fine grained and isotropic structure which provides excellent magnetic properties. Both types have their own unique applications and advantages in various industries.</p></p>
