<p><p>The Computer and Peripherals Standard Logic IC market comprises TTL IC (Transistor-transistor Logic), CMOS Logic IC (Complementary Metal Oxide Semiconductor), and BiCMOS Logic IC (Bipolar CMOS). TTL ICs offer high-speed operation and are commonly used in older computer systems. CMOS Logic ICs consume less power and are widely used in modern devices. BiCMOS Logic ICs combine the advantages of both TTL and CMOS, offering high-speed performance with low power consumption. Each type of logic IC caters to different requirements in the computer and peripherals market.</p></p>
