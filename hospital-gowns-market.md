<p><p>Hospital gowns are available in two main types: disposable and reusable. Disposable gowns are single-use garments made from lightweight materials that are typically discarded after each patient use, reducing the risk of cross-contamination. Reusable gowns, on the other hand, are made from durable fabrics that can withstand multiple washings and sterilizations, making them a more sustainable option for healthcare facilities. Both types of gowns play a crucial role in maintaining patient safety and hygiene in medical settings.</p></p>
