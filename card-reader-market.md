<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Card Reader Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Contact Card Reader</li><li>Non-Contact Card Reader</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Contact card readers require physical contact with the card for data transfer, such as swiping or inserting a card into a slot. These are commonly used in ATMs and credit card terminals. Non-contact card readers use radio frequency or infrared technology to read data from a card without physical contact. These are often seen in public transportation systems for ticketing or access control. Both types of card readers provide secure and efficient ways to retrieve information from cards in various applications.</p></p>
