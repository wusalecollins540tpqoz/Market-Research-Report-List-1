<p><strong>The Forging Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Closed Die Forgings</li><li>Open Die Forgings</li><li>Rolled Rings Forgings</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Closed Die Forgings involve compressing a piece of metal between two dies to achieve the desired shape. Open Die Forgings involve shaping metal by hammering or pressing between multiple dies with no enclosed cavity. Rolled Rings Forgings involve shaping a circular metal piece into a ring shape using rolling and pressing techniques. Each market type caters to specific needs and requirements, offering a range of advantages in terms of strength, durability, and customization options for various industries.</p></p>
