<p><p>Air Entraining Agents (AEA) are chemicals added to concrete mixes to create microscopic air bubbles, which improve durability and workability. There are two main types of AEA markets - those based on powdered surfactants and those based on synthetic surfactants. AEA based on powdered surfactants are preferred for their cost-effectiveness and ease of handling. On the other hand, AEA based on synthetic surfactants are known for their superior performance and consistency in air entrainment properties. Both types play a crucial role in enhancing the overall quality of concrete.</p></p>
