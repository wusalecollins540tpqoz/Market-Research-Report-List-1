<p><p>Aluminum flux comes in various types, with resin-based and water-soluble being two common varieties. Resin-based flux contains organic binders that help in the removal of oxides and impurities from the metal surface during the welding or soldering process. On the other hand, water-soluble flux dissolves easily in water after use, making it easier to clean the metal surface. Both types of flux are widely used in industries such as metal fabrication, electronics, and automotive manufacturing for their effectiveness in improving joint quality and reducing defects.</p></p>
