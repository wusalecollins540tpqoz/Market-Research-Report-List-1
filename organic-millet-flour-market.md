<p><p>Organic millet flour is available in the market in two main types - whole grain and others. Whole grain organic millet flour is made from the entire grain of millet, including the bran, germ, and endosperm, providing a high nutritional value. On the other hand, other types of organic millet flour may be processed to remove some parts of the grain, resulting in a different texture or flavor. Consumers can choose the type of organic millet flour based on their preferences and dietary needs.</p></p>
