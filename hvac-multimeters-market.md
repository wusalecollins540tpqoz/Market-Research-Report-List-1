<p><p>HVAC Multimeters come in two main types: digital and analog. A digital multimeter provides precise measurements displayed on a digital screen, making it easy to read and interpret data. On the other hand, an analog multimeter uses a needle or dial to show measurements, offering a more traditional approach to readings. Both types have their advantages, with digital multimeters being more accurate and easier to use, while analog multimeters are often preferred for their simplicity and durability.</p></p>
