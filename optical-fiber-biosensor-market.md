<p><p>Optical Fiber Biosensors are used in various applications due to their high sensitivity and rapid response. The Evanescent Wave Fluorescence Biosensor type utilizes the evanescent field to excite fluorophores near the fiber surface for sensitive molecular detection. Bioluminescent Optical Fiber Biosensors use bioluminescent reactions to measure biological analytes. Other types of Optical Fiber Biosensors include those that use acoustic, interferometric, or surface plasmon resonance principles. These biosensors offer diverse options for detecting a wide range of analytes in various environments.</p></p>
