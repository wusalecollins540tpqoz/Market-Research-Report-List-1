<p><ul><li>Hyperfiltration</li><li>Microfiltration</li><li>Nanofiltration</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Ceramic membrane market types include hyperfiltration, microfiltration, and nanofiltration. Hyperfiltration, also known as reverse osmosis, is used for separating small molecules from water. Microfiltration is used to remove larger particles and microorganisms from liquids. Nanofiltration is a process that separates even smaller particles and molecules from a liquid solution. These different types of ceramic membranes offer varying levels of filtration efficiency and are used in industries such as water treatment, food and beverage production, and pharmaceuticals.</p></p>
