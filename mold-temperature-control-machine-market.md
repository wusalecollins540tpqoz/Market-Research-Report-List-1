<p><p>Mold temperature control machines are equipment used in industrial processes to control the temperature of molds during manufacturing. There are two main types of these machines - water type temperature controllers and oil type temperature controllers. Water type controllers use water as the cooling medium, offering efficient and cost-effective temperature control. On the other hand, oil type controllers use oil as the cooling medium, providing more precise temperature control at higher temperatures. Both types play a crucial role in maintaining consistent and optimal mold temperatures for quality production.</p></p>
