<p><strong>The Enterprise External Storage Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>All Flash Array(AFA)</li><li>Hybrid Flash Array(HFA)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Enterprise External Storage market is divided into two main types - All Flash Array (AFA) and Hybrid Flash Array (HFA). AFA solely relies on flash storage for high performance, low latency, and reliable data storage. On the other hand, HFA combines both flash and traditional spinning drives to balance performance and cost. AFAs are preferred for performance-sensitive applications, while HFAs are chosen for a balance between performance and cost-effectiveness. Both types cater to different needs within the enterprise storage market.</p></p>
