<p><p>Leaded Multilayer Ceramic Capacitors are available in two main types: Radial Lead Type and Axial Lead Type. Radial Lead Type capacitors have leads that come out from one end of the capacitor body in a radial direction, while Axial Lead Type capacitors have leads that come out from opposite ends of the capacitor body in an axial direction. Both types offer easy installation and are commonly used in various electronic devices for their high capacitance and voltage tolerance.</p></p>
