<p><p>Broadband routers can be categorized into two main types: wired routers and wireless routers. Wired routers connect devices through Ethernet cables, providing stable and secure internet connections. In contrast, wireless routers use Wi-Fi technology to allow devices to connect without the need for cables, offering more flexibility and convenience. Both types cater to different needs and preferences, with wired routers being preferred for high-speed, reliable connections and wireless routers being popular for their ease of use and mobility.</p></p>
