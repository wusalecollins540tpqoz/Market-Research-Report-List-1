<p><strong>The Truck Clutch Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Friction Clutch</li><li>Electromagnetic Clutch</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The truck clutch market consists of two main types: friction clutches and electromagnetic clutches. Friction clutches utilize frictional force to engage and disengage the clutch when necessary. They are commonly used in manual transmission trucks. On the other hand, electromagnetic clutches operate using an electromagnetic field to control the engagement of the clutch. They are often found in automated and semi-automated transmission systems in trucks. Both types play a crucial role in ensuring smooth and efficient operation of trucks on the road.</p></p>
