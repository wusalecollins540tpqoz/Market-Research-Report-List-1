<p><p>Descalers are products used to remove limescale and calcium deposits from appliances like coffee makers, kettles, and washing machines. They come in three main types: gel, liquid, and powder. Gel descalers are thicker and easier to apply on vertical surfaces. Liquid descalers are versatile and can easily penetrate hard-to-reach areas. Powder descalers are concentrated and often require mixing with water before use. Each type has its own benefits and specific uses, catering to different preferences and needs in the market.</p></p>
