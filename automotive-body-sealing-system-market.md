<p><p>The Automotive Body Sealing System Market consists of three main types of materials: EPDM, TPE, and PVC. EPDM (Ethylene Propylene Diene Monomer) is a synthetic rubber known for its durability and weather resistance, making it ideal for sealing applications in vehicles. TPE (Thermoplastic Elastomer) combines the characteristics of rubber with the processability of plastic, providing a good balance of flexibility and strength. PVC (Polyvinyl Chloride) is a widely used plastic material known for its affordability and resistance to chemical corrosion. Each material offers different benefits for automotive sealing systems.</p></p>
