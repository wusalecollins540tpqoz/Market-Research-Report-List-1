<p><p>The automotive premium tires market includes two main types: Bias tires and Radial tires. Bias tires are constructed with layers of fabric crisscrossed at angles, providing higher durability and stability at high speeds. Radial tires, on the other hand, have layers of fabric arranged at 90 degrees from the direction of travel, resulting in better grip, fuel efficiency, and overall performance. Both types offer superior quality and are popular choices for premium automotive applications.</p></p>
