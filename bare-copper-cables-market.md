<p><p>Bare copper cables are available in three main market types: soft drawn, medium hard drawn, and hard drawn. Soft drawn cables are flexible and easily bendable, making them ideal for applications where frequent movement is required. Medium hard drawn cables are slightly more rigid than soft drawn cables, providing better resistance to wear and tear. Hard drawn cables are the most rigid and durable option, suitable for heavy-duty applications where strength and stability are crucial. Each market type offers varying levels of flexibility and durability to meet specific industry needs.</p></p>
