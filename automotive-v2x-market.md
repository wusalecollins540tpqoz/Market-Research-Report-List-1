<p><strong>The Automotive V2X Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>DSRC</li><li>Cellular</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive V2X (vehicle-to-everything) technology encompasses two main market types: Dedicated Short-Range Communications (DSRC) and Cellular. DSRC utilizes a specific wireless spectrum for direct vehicle-to-vehicle and vehicle-to-infrastructure communication. On the other hand, Cellular V2X relies on existing cellular networks to enable similar communication capabilities. While DSRC is more established and widely used, Cellular V2X is seen as the future due to its potential for broader coverage and integration with 5G technology. Both market types offer valuable solutions for enhancing road safety and traffic efficiency.</p></p>
