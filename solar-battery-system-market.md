<p><strong>The Solar Battery System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>DC coupled systems</li><li>AC coupled systems</li><li>AC Battery Systems</li><li>Hybrid Inverter Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>DC coupled systems are solar battery systems where the batteries are directly connected to the solar panels using a charge controller, enabling efficient energy storage. AC coupled systems involve the batteries being connected to the home's AC electrical system, allowing for greater flexibility and compatibility with existing infrastructure. AC Battery Systems are standalone units that can be easily integrated with any solar setup. Hybrid Inverter Systems combine the functions of a solar inverter and battery inverter in one unit, optimizing energy production and storage.</p></p>
