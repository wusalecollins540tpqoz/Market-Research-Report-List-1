<p><p>In-line valves are commonly used in various industries and can be classified into three main types: manual valves, pneumatic valves, and electromagnetic valves. Manual valves require manual operation to control the flow of fluids or gases. Pneumatic valves use air pressure to automate the valve opening and closing process. Electromagnetic valves utilize electrical currents to control the valve's operation. Each type of in-line valve offers unique advantages and is suitable for different applications based on the specific requirements of the system.</p></p>
