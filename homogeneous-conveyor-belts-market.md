<p><p>Homogeneous conveyor belts are made from various materials, with Thermoplastic Polyester (TPE) and Thermoplastic Polyurethane (TPU) being the most common types. TPE belts are known for their resistance to oil, grease, and abrasion, making them suitable for heavy-duty applications. On the other hand, TPU belts offer superior resistance to cuts, tears, and chemicals, making them ideal for food processing and pharmaceutical industries. Other materials used in homogeneous conveyor belts include PVC and rubber, each offering unique properties for specific applications.</p></p>
