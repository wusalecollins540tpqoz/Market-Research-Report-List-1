<p><p>Capnography equipments market includes three main types: Mainstream Capnography, Sidestream Capnography, and Microstream Capnography. Mainstream Capnography devices directly measure the CO2 levels in the airway, while Sidestream Capnography devices use a small tube to sample the exhaled air. Microstream Capnography utilizes a micro sample cell technology for accurate and fast monitoring of CO2 levels. Each type has its own advantages and applications, catering to different needs in various healthcare settings such as hospitals, clinics, and ambulances.</p></p>
