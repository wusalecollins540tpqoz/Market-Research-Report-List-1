<p><p>Citrus juices market is divided into two main types - Powdered Concentrate and Frozen Concentrate. Powdered concentrate is made by dehydrating citrus juice into a powder form, which can then be reconstituted with water before consumption. On the other hand, frozen concentrate involves removing water from the citrus juice and freezing it into a condensed form, which can be thawed and mixed with water before drinking. Both types offer convenience and long shelf life, making them popular choices among consumers.</p></p>
