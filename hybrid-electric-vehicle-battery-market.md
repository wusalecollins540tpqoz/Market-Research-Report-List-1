<p><p>A hybrid electric vehicle (HEV) battery is a rechargeable energy storage system that powers the electric motor of a hybrid vehicle to supplement the internal combustion engine. These batteries are usually nickel-metal hydride (NiMH) or lithium-ion (Li-ion) batteries, providing improved fuel efficiency and lower emissions compared to traditional gasoline vehicles. HEV batteries are essential components in hybrid vehicles, allowing them to operate in electric mode and store regenerated energy from braking.</p><p>The Hybrid Electric Vehicle Battery Market is expected to experience substantial growth, with a projected CAGR of 13.3% during the forecast period.
