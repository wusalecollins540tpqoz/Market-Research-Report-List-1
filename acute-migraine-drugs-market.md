<p><p>The Acute Migraine Drugs Market is segmented into three main types: Triptans, NSAIDs, and Others. Triptans are a class of drugs that target serotonin receptors to alleviate migraine symptoms. NSAIDs are nonsteroidal anti-inflammatory drugs that reduce inflammation and pain associated with migraines. Other types of drugs used for acute migraines include antiemetics, corticosteroids, and ergot alkaloids. Each type of drug has unique mechanisms of action and is used depending on the severity and individual response to treatment.</p></p>
