Fluorescent lamps are cost-effective and energy-efficient but provide lower light intensity compared to other options. High-Intensity Discharge (HID) lights are known for their high light output and are commonly used in commercial horticulture settings. LED lights are gaining popularity due to their energy efficiency, long lifespan, and customizable spectrum options. Each type of lighting has its own set of benefits and is chosen based on the specific requirements of the horticultural operation.</p></p>
