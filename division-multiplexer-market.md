<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Division Multiplexer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>FDM</li><li>WDM</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Division Multiplexer market types include Frequency Division Multiplexer (FDM) and Wavelength Division Multiplexer (WDM). FDM is used to combine multiple signals into a single channel by assigning each signal a different frequency within the channel. WDM, on the other hand, combines multiple signals on different wavelengths within a single fiber optic cable. FDM is commonly used in analog telecommunications while WDM is prevalent in high-speed data transmission systems. Both technologies play a crucial role in maximizing the efficiency and capacity of communication networks.</p></p>
