<p><p>High Pressure Fixed Firefighting Misting Systems are designed to operate at a pressure above 1000 psi, providing a fine mist that effectively suppresses fires. These systems are ideal for industrial and commercial settings where high fire risks are present. Non-high Pressure Fixed Firefighting Misting Systems operate at lower pressures and are more commonly used in residential buildings or smaller commercial spaces. Both types of systems use misting nozzles to disperse water droplets that quickly cool and extinguish fires.</p></p>
