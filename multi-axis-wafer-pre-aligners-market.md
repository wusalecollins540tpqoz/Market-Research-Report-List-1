<p><ul><li>Vacuum Aligners</li><li>Atmosphere Aligners</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Multi-axis wafer pre-aligners are used for precise alignment of wafers before processing in semiconductor manufacturing. Vacuum aligners create a vacuum chamber to hold the wafer in place for alignment, while atmosphere aligners use atmospheric pressure to secure the wafer during alignment. Vacuum aligners are preferred for better grip and stability, particularly for thinner wafers, while atmosphere aligners are suitable for more delicate handling. Both types play a crucial role in ensuring accurate alignment and processing of wafers in semiconductor fabrication.</p></p>
