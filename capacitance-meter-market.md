<p><strong>The Capacitance Meter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Non-bridge Meters</li><li>Bridges Meters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Capacitance meters are categorized into two main types: Non-bridge meters and bridge meters. Non-bridge meters measure capacitance without the need for a bridge circuit, making them simpler and more cost-effective. Bridge meters, on the other hand, use a bridge circuit to accurately measure capacitance by comparing it to a known standard. Bridge meters are typically more accurate and precise than non-bridge meters, making them the preferred choice for more advanced applications in industries such as electronics and telecommunications.</p></p>
