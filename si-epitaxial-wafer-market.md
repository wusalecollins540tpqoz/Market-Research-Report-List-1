<p><strong>The Si Epitaxial Wafer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Homoepitaxial Epitaxial Wafer</li><li>Heteroepitaxial Epitaxial Wafer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Si epitaxial wafer market can be divided into two main types - homoepitaxial epitaxial wafer and heteroepitaxial epitaxial wafer. Homoepitaxial epitaxial wafers are made by depositing Si on a substrate that has the same crystal structure as the Si. Heteroepitaxial epitaxial wafers, on the other hand, are made by depositing Si on a substrate with a different crystal structure. Both types of epitaxial wafer offer specific advantages and are used in various industries such as semiconductor manufacturing and electronic devices.</p></p>
