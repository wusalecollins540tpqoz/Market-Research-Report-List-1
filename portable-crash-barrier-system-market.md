<p><p>The Portable Crash Barrier System market offers three main types - Rigid, Semi-rigid, and Flexible barriers. Rigid barriers provide maximum protection due to their strong structure. Semi-rigid barriers offer a balance of protection and flexibility, able to absorb some impact energy. Flexible barriers are designed to deflect and absorb impact energy, reducing the severity of crashes.
