<p><p>Bus tires are primarily categorized into two types: bias tire and radial tire market. Bias tires are constructed with multiple layers of overlapping nylon cords, offering a sturdy and durable option for heavy-duty vehicles like buses. On the other hand, radial tires are made with layers of steel belts running perpendicular to the direction of travel, providing better grip, fuel efficiency, and longer tread life. Both options cater to different needs and preferences, ensuring a suitable choice for various bus operators.</p></p>
