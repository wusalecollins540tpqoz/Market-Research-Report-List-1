<p><p>The automotive adaptive cruise control system market includes both laser-based and radar-based technologies. Laser-based systems use light sensors to detect objects in front of the vehicle and adjust speed accordingly. Radar-based systems, on the other hand, use radio waves to measure the distance between the vehicle and objects in its path. Both types of systems offer drivers the convenience of maintaining a safe following distance from other vehicles on the road, enhancing overall driving experience and safety.</p></p>
