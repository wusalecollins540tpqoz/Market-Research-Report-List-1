<p><strong>The Steel Wire Rope Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Left Regular Lay</li><li>Left Lang Lay</li><li>Right Regular Lay</li><li>Right Lang Lay</li><li>Alternate Lay</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Steel wire rope market types refer to the different winding configurations of the wires in the rope. Left regular lay features strands wound in the same direction as the rope, while left lang lay has strands wound in the opposite direction. Right regular lay and right lang lay are similar to their left counterparts but with the strands wound in the opposite direction. Alternate lay involves a combination of regular and lang lay. These variations affect the flexibility, strength, and durability of the steel wire rope for different applications.</p></p>
