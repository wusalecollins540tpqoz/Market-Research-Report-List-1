<p><p>Manual dental sandblasters come in various types, based on the number of tanks they feature. A 2-tank sandblaster consists of two separate tanks, one for holding the abrasive material and the other for air pressure. Similarly, a 1-tank sandblaster has a single tank for both the abrasive material and air pressure. A 3-tank sandblaster includes an additional tank for water that can be mixed with the abrasive for polishing purposes. A 4-tank sandblaster has an extra tank for collecting waste material. Lastly, there are other sandblasters available in the market that may have additional features or functionalities beyond the tanks mentioned.</p></p>
