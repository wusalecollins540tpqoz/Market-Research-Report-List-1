<p><p>Alcohol breath analyzers are used to measure blood alcohol content levels in individuals. There are three main types of alcohol breath analyzers: fuel cell, semiconductor, and infrared. Fuel cell analyzers operate by measuring the electrical current produced when alcohol comes into contact with a catalyst. Semiconductor analyzers use a chemical reaction to detect alcohol vapors, while infrared analyzers measure the amount of light absorbed by alcohol molecules. Each type of analyzer has its own unique benefits and limitations, making them suitable for different applications.</p></p>
