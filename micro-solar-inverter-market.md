<p><p>Micro solar inverters are classified into three main types based on the number of phases they support. Single-phase inverters are designed for residential and small commercial applications. Three-phase inverters are used in larger commercial and industrial installations where a three-phase power supply is common. Multiphase inverters, on the other hand, are more advanced and can support multiple phases, making them suitable for complex grid configurations and high-power applications. Each type of inverter offers unique capabilities to meet different energy needs and system requirements.</p></p>
