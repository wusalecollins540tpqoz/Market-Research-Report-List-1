<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Resistors for Electric Vehicles Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Shunt Resistors</li><li>Voltage Limiting Resistors</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Shunt resistors are used in electric vehicles to measure the current flowing through a circuit. They provide a low-resistance path for current to flow, allowing for accurate current monitoring. Voltage limiting resistors are used to protect sensitive components in electric vehicles from damage due to excessive voltage levels. They limit the amount of voltage that can pass through a circuit, ensuring the components are not overloaded. Other markets for resistors in electric vehicles may include temperature sensing resistors, noise suppression resistors, and surge protection resistors.</p></p>
