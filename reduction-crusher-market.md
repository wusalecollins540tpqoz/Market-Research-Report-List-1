<p><ul><li>Primary Reduction Crushers</li><li>Secondary Reduction Crushers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Reduction crushers are commonly categorized into two main types: primary reduction crushers and secondary reduction crushers. Primary reduction crushers are designed to initially break down large chunks of material into smaller pieces, whereas secondary reduction crushers further refine the crushed material into the desired final size. Primary reduction crushers are typically used at the beginning of the crushing process, while secondary reduction crushers are employed towards the end to achieve the desired size and shape of the final product.</p></p>
