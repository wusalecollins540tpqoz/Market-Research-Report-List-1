<p><p>Aluminum Conductors market is categorized into three main types - GJ, LGJ, and LGJF. GJ conductors are mainly used for overhead power transmission lines due to their high strength and durability. LGJ conductors are light weight and flexible, making them ideal for distribution lines. LGJF conductors are designed for special applications where corrosion resistance is required, such as in coastal areas. Each type of conductor has specific characteristics that make them suitable for different electrical transmission and distribution purposes.</p></p>
