<p><p>Swipe sensors and area sensors are two main types of screen fingerprint sensors in the market. Swipe sensors require the user to swipe their finger across a designated area on the screen for authentication. On the other hand, area sensors only require the user to place their finger on a specific area of the screen to unlock the device. Both types offer secure and convenient ways for users to access their devices, with area sensors being more popular due to their ease of use.</p></p>
