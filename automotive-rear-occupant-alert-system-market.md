<p><p>The Automotive Rear Occupant Alert System market consists of two main types: Ultrasonic Sensor System and Pressure Sensor System. The Ultrasonic Sensor System uses sound waves to detect the presence of occupants in the rear seats, while the Pressure Sensor System measures weight distribution to identify if someone is seated there. Both systems are designed to alert drivers when there are occupants in the back of the vehicle, helping to prevent accidents and ensure the safety of rear passengers.</p></p>
