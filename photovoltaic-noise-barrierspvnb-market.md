Conventional PVNB feature solar panels on one side to absorb sunlight and produce electricity. Bifacial PVNB, on the other hand, have solar panels on both sides, allowing them to capture sunlight from both directions. This design increases energy generation efficiency. Both types of PVNB offer a sustainable solution for noise pollution control while generating clean energy simultaneously.</p></p>
