<p><ul><li>Infrared</li><li>M Wave</li><li>Decimeter Wave</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The wireless headset for gaming market includes three main types based on the technology used for transmission: Infrared, M Wave, and Decimeter Wave. Infrared technology uses light signals to transmit audio, providing a stable connection but with limited range. M Wave technology uses microwave frequencies for transmission, offering a balance between range and stability. Decimeter Wave technology operates at higher frequencies than M Wave, enabling faster data transfer rates and better audio quality, making it suitable for high-end gaming experiences.</p></p>
