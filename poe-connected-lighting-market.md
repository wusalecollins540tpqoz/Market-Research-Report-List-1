<p><ul><li>Powered Device Controllers and Ics</li><li>Power Sourcing Equipment Controllers and Ics</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The PoE Connected Lighting Market can be classified into two main types: Powered Device Controllers and ICs, and Power Sourcing Equipment Controllers and ICs. Powered Device Controllers and ICs are devices that receive power and control signals from the Power Sourcing Equipment, allowing the lighting system to operate efficiently. On the other hand, Power Sourcing Equipment Controllers and ICs manage the distribution of power and control signals to the Powered Device, ensuring reliable and stable operation of the connected lighting system.</p></p>
