<p><strong>The Decorative Coatings Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Primer</li><li>Enamel</li><li>Emulsions</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The decorative coatings market consists of three main types: primer, enamel, and emulsions. Primers are used as a preparatory layer before applying paint or other coatings to enhance adhesion and durability. Enamels provide a smooth, glossy finish and are often used for high-visibility or high-traffic areas due to their excellent resistance to wear and tear. Emulsions are water-based paints that offer good coverage and can be easily cleaned, making them suitable for both interior and exterior applications. These coatings play a significant role in enhancing the aesthetic appeal and protection of various surfaces.</p></p>
