<p><p>LED Dermatoscopes are medical devices used by dermatologists for skin examination. There are two main types of LED Dermatoscopes available in the market: Contact Dermatoscopes and Noncontact Dermatoscopes. Contact Dermatoscopes require direct contact with the skin for examination, providing high-resolution images. Noncontact Dermatoscopes, on the other hand, use optical technology to capture images without touching the skin, allowing for a more comfortable and hygienic examination experience. Both types of LED Dermatoscopes are essential tools for accurate diagnosis and monitoring of skin conditions.</p></p>
