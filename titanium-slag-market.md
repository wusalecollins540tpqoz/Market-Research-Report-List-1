<p><p>Titanium slag is a byproduct of titanium dioxide production, classified into two main types - Acid Soluble Slag and Chlorination Slag. Acid Soluble Slag is produced through a chemical method that allows for easy extraction of titanium and other valuable elements. Both types have unique characteristics and applications in various industries, making them valuable commodities in the market.</p></p>
