<p><p>Touchless water dispensers offer various types of technology to provide a hands-free experience. Foot pedal-operated water dispensers utilize a pedal to dispense water without needing to touch the device. Position Sensitive Detector (PSD) water dispensers use sensors to detect hand movement and dispense water accordingly. Phone App/QR Code water dispensers allow users to control the dispenser through a smartphone application or by scanning a QR code. Paddle water dispensers feature a paddle that can be pressed with the elbow to dispense water without using hands.</p></p>
