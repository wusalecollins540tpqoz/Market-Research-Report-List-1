<p><p>Counterflow dryers are widely used in various industries for efficient drying of materials. There are two main types of counterflow dryers-- batch dryers and continuous dryers. Batch dryers are used for smaller quantities of materials and are operated in batches, while continuous dryers are used for larger quantities and operate continuously. Both types of dryers use the counterflow principle, where the hot air flows in the opposite direction of the material being dried, maximizing heat transfer efficiency and ensuring uniform drying.</p></p>
