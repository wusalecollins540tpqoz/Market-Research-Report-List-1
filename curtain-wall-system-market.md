<p><ul><li>Component Curtain Wall</li><li>Pressed Component Curtain Wall</li><li>Hinged Component Curtain Wall</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Curtain wall system market can be categorized into three types: component curtain wall, pressed component curtain wall, and hinged component curtain wall. Component curtain walls are made up of individual components that are assembled on site. Pressed component curtain walls are pre-fabricated panels that are pressed into shape before installation. Hinged component curtain walls are panels that are pre-assembled and hinged together for easier installation. Each type offers different benefits in terms of customization, speed of installation, and cost-effectiveness.</p></p>
