<p><p>Condenser microphones can be categorized into three main types: Large-Diaphragm Condenser, Small-Diaphragm Condenser, and Others such as Lavalier microphones. Large-Diaphragm Condenser mics are known for their warm, rich sound quality and are commonly used in studio recording. Small-Diaphragm Condenser mics offer more detailed sound reproduction and are often used for capturing acoustic instruments. Lavalier microphones are small, discreet mics commonly used in broadcasting and public speaking situations. Each type caters to specific recording needs and preferences.</p></p>
