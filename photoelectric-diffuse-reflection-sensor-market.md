<p><p>Photoelectric diffuse reflection sensors are categorized into two types based on their output transistor: PNP and NPN. PNP sensors provide a positive output signal when the object is detected, while NPN sensors provide a negative output signal. Both types are commonly used in automation and industrial applications for detecting the presence or absence of objects. PNP sensors are often preferred in applications where a positive signal is needed, while NPN sensors are commonly used in applications where a negative signal is acceptable.</p></p>
