<p><p>Dedicated Short Range Communication (DSRC) technology is designed for communication over short distances, such as vehicle-to-vehicle or vehicle-to-infrastructure. This type of market focuses on direct, immediate communication in close proximity. In contrast, Long Range/Cellular Network technology utilizes cellular networks to enable communication over longer distances. This market type allows for broader coverage and connectivity, supporting functions like remote diagnostics and fleet management. Both types play a vital role in the connected truck market, catering to different communication needs and capabilities.</p></p>
