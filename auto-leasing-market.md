<p><p>Auto leasing market is divided into three main types: Short-term Rental, Long-term Rental, and Finance Leasing. Short-term Rental involves leasing a vehicle for a few days to a few weeks, ideal for travelers or temporary needs. Long-term Rental offers leasing options for a few months up to a year, suited for extended trips or temporary work assignments. Finance Leasing, on the other hand, involves longer contracts, typically 2-5 years, where the lessee pays for the depreciation of the vehicle and has the option to purchase it at the end of the term.</p></p>
