<p><ul><li>Slotted Wire Duct</li><li>Solid-wall Wire Duct</li><li>Flexible Wire Duct</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Wire ducts are commonly categorized into four types in the market: slotted wire duct, solid-wall wire duct, flexible wire duct, and others. Slotted wire ducts have openings along the walls for easy cable insertion and removal. Solid-wall wire ducts have no openings, providing better protection for cables. Flexible wire ducts are bendable and ideal for tight spaces or complex cable routing. The "others" category includes unique wire duct designs that may not fit into the standard slotted or solid-wall categories.</p></p>
