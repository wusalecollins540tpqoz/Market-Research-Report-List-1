<p><ul><li>Vacuum Cluster Tool</li><li>Non-Vacuum Cluster Tool</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Cluster tools are used in semiconductor manufacturing to improve production efficiency. Vacuum cluster tools utilize vacuum chambers and robotic arms to handle sensitive components without contamination. Non-vacuum cluster tools, on the other hand, use atmospheric pressure for processing. Both types of cluster tools offer high throughput and automation capabilities to meet the demands of the semiconductor industry. Companies can choose between vacuum and non-vacuum cluster tools based on their specific requirements and production processes.</p></p>
