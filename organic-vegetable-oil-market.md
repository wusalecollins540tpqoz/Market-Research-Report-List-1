<p><p>Organic vegetable oil is typically categorized into two main market types: food grade and industrial grade. Food grade organic vegetable oil is intended for consumption and is used in cooking, baking, and food manufacturing. On the other hand, industrial grade organic vegetable oil is primarily used in non-food applications such as cosmetics, pharmaceuticals, and biofuel production. While food grade organic vegetable oil must meet strict quality and safety standards for human consumption, industrial grade oil is often used for its chemical properties rather than its taste or nutritional value.</p></p>
