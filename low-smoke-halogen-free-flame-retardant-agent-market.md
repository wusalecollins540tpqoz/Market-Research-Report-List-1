Nitrogen Flame Retardants release ammonia or melamine when heated, diluting flammable gases. Silicon Flame Retardants form a protective layer of silica when exposed to heat. Aluminum-magnesium Flame Retardants release water vapor and carbon dioxide when heated, suppressing combustion. Each type offers unique benefits in terms of fire safety and environmental friendliness.</p></p>
