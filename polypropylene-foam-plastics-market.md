<p><p>Polypropylene Foam Plastics market can be categorized into two main types - Extruded Polypropylene Foam Plastics and Expanded Polypropylene Foam Plastics. Extruded Polypropylene Foam Plastics are created through a process where the material is forced through a die to create a uniform product with a smooth surface. Expanded Polypropylene Foam Plastics, on the other hand, are produced by expanding beads of polypropylene in a mold, resulting in a lightweight material with excellent cushioning properties. Both types find applications in various industries due to their unique properties.</p></p>
