<p><p>Mechanical Suspension Seats in the tractor seats market utilize a system of springs and dampers to absorb shocks and vibrations, providing a comfortable ride for the operator. They are cost-effective and easy to maintain. On the other hand, Air Suspension Seats use compressed air to create a cushioning effect, offering superior comfort and adjustment options for different weights and road conditions. They are considered more advanced and provide better support for the operator during long hours of work.</p></p>
