<p><p>Amino-terminated polyether is produced using two main methods: the intermittent method and the continuous method. In the intermittent method, the production process is carried out in batches, with pauses between each batch. This method is suitable for small-scale production. On the other hand, the continuous method involves a continuous flow of production without any breaks, making it more efficient for large-scale production. Both methods have their advantages and are used in different circumstances based on the scale of production required.</p></p>
