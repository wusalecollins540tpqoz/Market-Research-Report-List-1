<p><strong>The Laser-based Gas Analyzers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Tunable Diode Laser Absorption Spectroscopy</li><li>Raman Analyzers</li><li>Cavity-Ring Down Spectroscopy</li><li>Quantum Cascade Laser Spectroscopy</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Laser-based gas analyzers are cutting-edge technologies used to analyze gas components in various industries. Tunable Diode Laser Absorption Spectroscopy utilizes a tunable diode laser to measure gas concentrations with high sensitivity and selectivity. Raman Analyzers use laser light to analyze gas samples based on their molecular vibrations. Cavity-Ring Down Spectroscopy measures gas concentrations by analyzing the decay of light in a high-finesse optical cavity. Quantum Cascade Laser Spectroscopy utilizes quantum cascade lasers to detect gases by measuring their absorption of infrared light.</p></p>
