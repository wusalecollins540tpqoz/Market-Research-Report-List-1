<p><ul><li>Resistive Type</li><li>Capacitive Induction Type</li><li>Infrared Type</li><li>Surface Acoustic Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Touch Screen Modules market is segmented into four main types: Resistive Type, Capacitive Induction Type, Infrared Type, and Surface Acoustic Type. Resistive Type screens rely on pressure to register touch, while Capacitive Induction Type screens detect changes in capacitance. Infrared Type screens use infrared light to detect touch, and Surface Acoustic Type screens use ultrasonic waves. Each type offers unique advantages and is suited for different applications, catering to diverse consumer needs in the touch screen market.</p></p>
