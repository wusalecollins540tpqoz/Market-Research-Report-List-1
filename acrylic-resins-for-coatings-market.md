<p><p>Acrylic resins for coatings are widely used in two main types of markets: water-based and solvent-based. Water-based acrylic resins are preferred for their low VOC emissions, easy cleanup, and environmental friendliness. They are commonly used in applications where odor and flammability are concerns. On the other hand, solvent-based acrylic resins offer excellent adhesion and durability, making them suitable for high-performance coatings in demanding environments. Both types of acrylic resins provide excellent protection and aesthetics for a variety of surfaces.</p></p>
