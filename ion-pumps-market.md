<p><strong>The Ion Pumps Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Conventional/Standard Diode Pump</li><li>Noble Diode Ion Pump</li><li>Triode Pump</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Ion pumps are used in vacuum systems to maintain low pressure by removing gas molecules. The three main types of ion pumps are Conventional/Standard Diode Pump, Noble Diode Ion Pump, and Triode Pump. Conventional/Standard Diode Pump is the simplest and most common type, using a single electrode to trap and expel gas molecules. Noble Diode Ion Pump utilizes noble gases to increase pump efficiency. Triode Pump includes an additional electrode to enhance ionization and pumping capacity, making it the most advanced and efficient type of ion pump.</p></p>
