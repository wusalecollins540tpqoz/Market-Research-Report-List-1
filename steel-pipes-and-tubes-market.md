<p><p>Steel pipes and tubes market can be categorized into seamless tube and welded tube segments. Seamless tubes are manufactured from a single piece of metal without any welding or joints, providing high strength and the ability to withstand high pressure. On the other hand, welded tubes are created by welding together multiple pieces of metal, offering cost-effective solutions for various applications. Both types of tubes are widely used in industries such as oil and gas, construction, automotive, and manufacturing.</p></p>
