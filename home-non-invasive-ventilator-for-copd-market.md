<p><p>Single-Level Ventilators are devices that provide a constant pressure for inhaling and exhaling air to help with breathing difficulties in COPD patients. On the other hand, Bilevel Ventilators offer two different levels of pressure - a higher pressure for inhaling and a lower pressure for exhaling. This allows for more effective ventilation support for COPD patients with varying respiratory needs. Both types of ventilators play a crucial role in managing COPD symptoms and improving quality of life for patients.</p></p>
