<p><p>Hollow core insulators are mainly classified into two types: ceramic and composite. On the other hand, composite hollow core insulators are made of a combination of materials such as silicone rubber and glass fiber, offering benefits such as lightweight design, high flexibility, and resistance to pollution. Both types of insulators play a crucial role in electrical systems for providing insulation and maintaining system reliability.</p></p>
