<p><p>Paintable silicone is available in two main market types - squeeze tube and cartridge. Squeeze tube paintable silicone is convenient for smaller projects and touch-ups, offering easy application with precise control over the amount dispensed. On the other hand, cartridge paintable silicone is typically used for larger projects and professional applications. It is compatible with caulking guns for efficient and uniform application. Both market types offer a durable and paintable sealant solution for various surfaces, providing versatility and performance in sealing and waterproofing applications.</p></p>
