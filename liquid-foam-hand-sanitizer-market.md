<p><p>Liquid foam hand sanitizers are available in two main types: Alcohol foam hand sanitizers and alcohol-free foam hand sanitizers. Alcohol foam hand sanitizers contain alcohol as the active ingredient, typically in concentrations ranging from 60-95%, which effectively kill germs and bacteria on hands. On the other hand, alcohol-free foam hand sanitizers use alternative antimicrobial agents like benzalkonium chloride to achieve the same germ-killing effect without the drying effects of alcohol. Both types offer convenient and effective hand sanitization for on-the-go use.</p></p>
