<p><p>The PCB transformer market offers various types to suit different needs. Dual Primary transformers have two separate input connections, while Dual Secondary transformers have two output connections. Single Primary, Dual Secondary transformers have one input and two output connections, making them suitable for connecting multiple devices. Single Primary, Single Secondary transformers have just one input and one output connection, commonly used in simpler electronic devices. Each type caters to specific requirements, offering versatility and flexibility in PCB transformer applications.</p></p>
