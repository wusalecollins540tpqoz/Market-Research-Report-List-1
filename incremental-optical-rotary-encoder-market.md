<p><p>An Incremental Optical Rotary Encoder is a type of encoder that converts angular position or motion of a shaft into an analog or digital signal. It consists of a light source, a disc with radial lines or slots, and a light sensor to detect the movement of the disc. The encoder provides information about the speed, direction, and position of the rotating shaft, making it useful in various applications such as robotics, automation, and industrial control systems.</p><p>The Incremental Optical Rotary Encoder Market is witnessing significant growth due to the increasing demand for precision motion control devices in industries such as automotive, electronics, and aerospace.
