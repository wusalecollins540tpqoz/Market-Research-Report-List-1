<p><ul><li>Electroplated Diamond Wire</li><li>Resin Diamond Wire</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Diamond wire market is segmented into two main types: Electroplated Diamond Wire and Resin Diamond Wire. Electroplated Diamond Wire is coated with nickel or other metals and is known for its high cutting efficiency and long lifespan. Resin Diamond Wire, on the other hand, is made with resin and diamond powder and is preferred for its flexibility and precision cutting. Both types of diamond wire are widely used in industries such as construction, mining, and electronics for cutting hard materials like granite, concrete, and semiconductors.</p></p>
