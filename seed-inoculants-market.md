<p><p>Seed inoculants are products containing beneficial microorganisms that are applied to seeds before planting to improve plant growth and yield. The three main types of seed inoculants are Bacterials, Fungal Inoculants, and Composite Inoculants. Bacterial inoculants contain nitrogen-fixing bacteria that help plants absorb nitrogen from the soil. Fungal inoculants contain symbiotic fungi that enhance nutrient uptake. Composite inoculants contain both bacterial and fungal components, providing a wide range of benefits for plant health and growth.</p></p>
