<p><strong>The Polyurethane Tires Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>PPG Polyurethane</li><li>PTMEG Polyurethane</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Polyurethane tires can be categorized into two main types based on the raw material used in their production: PPG polyurethane and PTMEG polyurethane. PPG polyurethane tires utilize polypropylene glycol as a key raw material, offering high performance and durability. On the other hand, PTMEG polyurethane tires use polytetramethylene ether glycol, providing excellent elasticity and resilience. Both types are widely used in various applications such as industrial equipment, automotive vehicles, and material handling machinery due to their long-lasting nature and superior performance.</p></p>
