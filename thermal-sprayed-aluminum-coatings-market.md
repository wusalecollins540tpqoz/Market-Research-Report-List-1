<p><p>Thermal Sprayed Aluminum Coatings are used in various industries for their corrosion resistance and thermal protection properties. Two common types of thermal spraying methods used for applying aluminum coatings are Flame Spraying and Arc Spraying. Flame Spraying involves heating aluminum wire and projecting it onto the substrate surface, while Arc Spraying involves melting aluminum wire through an electric arc and propelling it onto the surface. Both methods result in a durable and protective aluminum coating ideal for a range of applications.</p></p>
