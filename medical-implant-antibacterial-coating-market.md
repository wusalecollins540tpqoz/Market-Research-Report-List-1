<p><p>The medical implant antibacterial coating market can be categorized into metallic and non-metallic types. Metallic coatings, such as silver and copper, release ions that have antibacterial properties, helping to prevent infections. Non-metallic coatings, on the other hand, utilize materials like polymers or hydrogels to provide a barrier against bacterial adhesion and growth on the implant surface. Both types of coatings aim to improve the safety and efficacy of medical implants by reducing the risk of infections for patients.</p></p>
