<p><strong>The Coolant Temperature Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>2-Wire Coolant Temperature Sensors</li><li>1-Wire Coolant Temperature Sensors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Coolant Temperature Sensors are used in vehicles to monitor the temperature of the engine coolant. There are two main types in the market: 2-Wire Coolant Temperature Sensors and 1-Wire Coolant Temperature Sensors. </p><p>2-Wire sensors have a separate ground wire and signal wire, providing more accurate temperature readings. They are commonly used in newer vehicles. </p><p>In contrast, 1-Wire sensors have just one wire for both power and signal, making them simpler and less expensive. They are typically found in older vehicles or budget-friendly models.</p></p>
