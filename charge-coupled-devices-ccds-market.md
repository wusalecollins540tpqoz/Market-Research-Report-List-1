<p><strong>The Charge-coupled Devices (CCDs) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Line CCD</li><li>Interline CCD</li><li>Full-Frame CCD</li><li>Frame-Transfer CCD</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Charge-coupled devices (CCDs) have several market types including Line CCD, Interline CCD, Full-Frame CCD, and Frame-Transfer CCD. Line CCDs capture one row of pixels at a time, making them suitable for high-speed applications. Interline CCDs have a built-in transfer register, allowing for faster readout speeds. Full-Frame CCDs capture an entire image at once, offering high resolution. Frame-Transfer CCDs use a separate area for image capture and readout, enabling continuous operation without interruption. Each CCD type caters to different applications based on its specific design and capabilities.</p></p>
