<p><p>Poultry cage market offers two main types of cages: Fully automatic and semi-automatic. Fully automatic cages come equipped with features like automatic feeding, watering, egg collection, and manure removal systems, reducing the need for manual labor and increasing efficiency. Both types cater to different needs and budgets of poultry farmers, offering options for those looking for higher automation or more hands-on control.</p></p>
