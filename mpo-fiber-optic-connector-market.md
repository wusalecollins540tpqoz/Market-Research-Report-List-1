<p><strong>The MPO Fiber Optic Connector Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-Mode</li><li>Multi-Mode</li></ul></p>
<p><p>MPO fiber optic connectors are available in two market types: single-mode and multi-mode. Single-mode connectors are designed for long-distance transmission with a narrower core size, allowing a single mode of light to travel through with minimum dispersion. Multi-mode connectors have a larger core size, allowing multiple modes of light to travel through, making them suitable for shorter distances. Both types of connectors offer high-speed, reliable connections for various applications in telecommunications, data centers, and network infrastructure.</p></p>
