<p><ul><li>Preheating Chamber Furnace</li><li>Preheating Tunnel Furnace</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Preheating Chamber Furnace and Preheating Tunnel Furnace are two types of preheating furnaces used in various industries. </p><p>Preheating Chamber Furnace is a type of furnace where the material to be heated is placed in a chamber and heated using electric or gas heating elements. This type of furnace is suitable for smaller batch sizes and allows for precise temperature control.</p><p>Preheating Tunnel Furnace, on the other hand, is a continuous furnace where the material is continuously fed through a tunnel and heated as it passes through different heating zones. This type of furnace is ideal for high-volume production processes and offers efficient heating of materials.</p></p>
